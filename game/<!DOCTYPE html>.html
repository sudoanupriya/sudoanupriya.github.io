Jeopardy Game Instructions

Welcome to the Jeopardy game! Here are the instructions:

  1. Divide the players into two teams and choose a moderator to share their screen and present the game board.
  2. The game board will contain categories and point values for each question. Each team takes turns in choosing a category and point value.
  3. The moderator reads the corresponding question and the team has 30 seconds to discuss and submit their answer in the chat.
  4. If the team answers correctly, they earn the points for that question. If they answer incorrectly, the other team gets a chance to steal the points by providing the correct answer in the chat.
  5. The moderator will reveal the correct answer and award points accordingly.
  6. The other team takes a turn to choose a category and point value, and the game continues in this way until the designated time limit has elapsed.
  7. At the end of the game, the team with the most points is declared the winner.
